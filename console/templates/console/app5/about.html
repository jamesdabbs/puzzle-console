Auburn Puzzle Party 5 Puzzle Patrol II

Auburn Puzzle Party 5: Puzzle Patrol II is a puzzlehunt taking place in Auburn, AL, starting Saturday, October 20, 2012 at 10am and ending Sunday, October 21, 2012 at 2pm.

Wait, what's a "puzzlehunt"?

Good question! You're probably familiar with a "scavenger hunt", where you and a team of your friends are given a list of items to find. Your team then has to run around a designated area collecting or photographing those items, and the more items you find, the more points you earn.

A puzzlehunt is similar. The difference is, instead of finding stuff, you find and solve puzzles. Usually not jigsaw puzzles (although those aren't forbidden), but riddles or codes. Or perhaps logic or trivia-based puzzles. Really, there's not a specific "type" of puzzle you can expect to find, and that's part of the fun!

Essentially an adventure videogame brought to life, puzzlehunts can seem silly, but they are an incredible amount of fun, as well as a challenge! There's a whole community in Auburn, AL who plan and participate in these events (along with puzzle "parties" and "potlucks") - you can visit the Auburn Puzzle Party wiki to learn more.

Okay, tell me about APP5.

Auburn Puzzle Party 5: Puzzle Patrol II is the fifth community-organized puzzlehunt in Auburn. It is organized by most of the same people who brought you APP4: Auburn Puzzle Patrol in Fall 2010, and serves as a sequel. Don't worry, you don't need to be familiar with APP4 to enjoy playing in APP5, nor will it give anyone an unfair advantage!

How do I get involved?

If you'd like to play, but aren't sure where to begin, please email the event organizer, Steven Clontz and he'll be happy to chat with you. Or if you'd prefer, go to our Facebook event and make a post.

If you're a regular, note that we have designed an automated registration process on this website, rather than the usual email-the-organizer or edit-the-wiki methods. If you need help using the online registration, let Steven know. Only the captain of each team must register on this site - however, we recommend most if not all players register for the site, as only Players who have registered can access the Puzzle Console to view and submit solutions to puzzles.
