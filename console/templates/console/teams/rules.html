• Players are classified based on how many Trophies they have won at past Puzzle Parties:
    • Rookies are Players who have won no Trophies at past parties, including those who have not played in any past parties.
    • Heroes are Players who have only won a single Trophy at past parties.
    • Legends are Players who have won two or more Trophies at past parties.
    • Past players should have the appropriate classification associated with their name already on the Console. New players should be classified as Rookies when they are created on the Console. Email Steven with any errors.
  • Recreational Teams may have any number of players, regardless of classification. Recreational teams are only eligible for the "Best Recreational Team" award at the end of the game.
  • Competitive Teams are eligible for every other award, but are subject to the following restrictions:
    • Each team may have at most eight players. Seven or eight players is strongly recommended.
    • The number of Legends a Team can have is determined by the number of Rookies that have registered for that Team:
      • Any Team can have two Legends.
      • Teams with at least two Rookies can have three Legends.
      • Teams with four Rookies can have four Legends.
